Collaboration

Interaction between team members took place through communication in the Discord channel, periodic meetings in Google Meetup, and checking each other’s PR in Github.

When checking each other's PR, attention was paid to errors, ways to improve functionality, and the quality of code writing. Also, when checking, the Team Lead, being a native of the Java language, steadily imposed his vision of writing code in the OOP style.

During online meetings, issues of the visual side of the project and its interaction with logic were discussed. In addition to the discussion, layouts of pages drawn in non-programmer style in Paint and the distribution of tasks in Word were also offered here. Also, significant time at the meetings was devoted to discussing everyday issues, plans for the future and just for jokes.

Dividing the tasks of each stage into the areas of responsibility of each team member made it possible to speed up development and find strengths and weaknesses in coding for each team member.

As a result of team work was this application.
